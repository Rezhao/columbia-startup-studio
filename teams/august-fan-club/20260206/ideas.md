# Problem-Solution Pairs

## Problem-Solution Pair 1

**Context:** Stay-at-home mom · iPad · Apartment · Studying

1. **Problem:** No quiet place to study  
   **Why it matters:** Study time is limited and easily ruined by noise and interruptions.  
   **Solution:** “Focus Corner” mode with a 25-minute timer, noise-masking audio, auto Do Not Disturb, and a one-tap “resume later” that restores exact state.  
   **Pain likelihood:** 9/10

2. **Problem:** Constant interruptions make her lose her place  
   **Why it matters:** Re-reading wastes time and increases stress.  
   **Solution:** Auto “Where I left off” bookmark that snapshots page/scroll position and prompts a one-line note when locking the iPad.  
   **Pain likelihood:** 9/10

3. **Problem:** Kids touch the iPad and mess things up  
   **Why it matters:** Accidental deletes or purchases ruin precious study windows.  
   **Solution:** Study Lock: one gesture locks the current app, disables purchases, and blocks app switching without Face ID/passcode.  
   **Pain likelihood:** 8/10

4. **Problem:** Too many apps and materials, hard to stay organized  
   **Why it matters:** High mental load kills motivation.  
   **Solution:** A “Course Hub” home screen with one page per class containing pinned PDFs, notes, to-dos, and links.  
   **Pain likelihood:** 8/10

5. **Problem:** Studying while cooking or watching kids  
   **Why it matters:** Micro-study is often the only way to make progress.  
   **Solution:** Audio-first study mode with text-to-speech and voice commands (“pause,” “bookmark,” “repeat”).  
   **Pain likelihood:** 7/10

6. **Problem:** Apartment lighting causes eye strain  
   **Why it matters:** Headaches prevent long study sessions.  
   **Solution:** Eye-comfort preset with scheduled Night Shift, larger text, glare reminders, and 20-20-20 break nudges.  
   **Pain likelihood:** 7/10

7. **Problem:** Phone notifications break concentration  
   **Why it matters:** Context switching is expensive and fragile.  
   **Solution:** Study whitelist allowing only key contacts and childcare notifications.  
   **Pain likelihood:** 8/10

8. **Problem:** Note-taking is slow and messy  
   **Why it matters:** Exams require efficient review.  
   **Solution:** Structured note templates (Cornell/lecture) with auto “Key Terms” and end-of-session summaries.  
   **Pain likelihood:** 7/10

9. **Problem:** Unstable shared Wi-Fi  
   **Why it matters:** Tech issues waste short study windows.  
   **Solution:** Offline pack that downloads all materials needed for the next 24 hours.  
   **Pain likelihood:** 6/10

10. **Problem:** Guilt about studying instead of “being productive”  
    **Why it matters:** Guilt reduces consistency more than difficulty.  
    **Solution:** Progress proof dashboard showing streaks, minutes studied, and “what I learned today.”  
    **Pain likelihood:** 8/10
